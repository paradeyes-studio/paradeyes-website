# Template, Editorial Illustration (manifesto, about, abstract)

## Specifications techniques

- Aspect ratio, 16:9 ou 21:9 cinéma
- Resolution, 1920x1080 minimum
- Format de sortie, PNG → WebP optionnel
- Save path, `public/images/editorial/{slug}.png`

## Base prompt en anglais

Abstract editorial illustration in a premium minimalist style for a strategic communication agency.
Subject, abstract conceptual representation of {USER_BRIEF}, using geometric forms, light beams, soft volumetric atmospheres, or architectural fragments.
Composition, symmetric or rule-of-thirds, deep perspective, strong vanishing point.
Lighting, volumetric god rays, soft halos, atmospheric haze, deep shadows.
Color palette, dominant deep green #003135 atmosphere with electric green #57EEA1 accent light source, violet spark #6549F6 used very sparingly for energy points, warm white #FAFAF7 highlights.
Texture, subtle film grain, atmospheric particles, very fine grid overlay (optional, low opacity).
Mood, contemplative, futuristic-yet-timeless, intellectual, slightly mysterious.
Style references, Apple Vision Pro launch visuals, Stripe abstract illustrations, Anthropic brand visuals, Awwwards SOTD illustrations 2024.
NO realistic objects, NO branded items, NO human figures.
