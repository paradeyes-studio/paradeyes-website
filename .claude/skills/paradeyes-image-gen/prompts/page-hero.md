# Template, Page Hero (hero image pour pages principales)

## Specifications techniques

- Aspect ratio, 16:9 ou 21:9 cinéma wide
- Resolution, 2560x1080 minimum
- Format de sortie, PNG → WebP optionnel
- Save path, `public/images/heroes/{page}.png`

## Base prompt en anglais

Cinematic hero image for a premium creative communication agency website.
Subject, abstract or environmental representation of {USER_BRIEF}.
Composition, wide cinematic frame, layered depth (foreground, midground, background), strong focal point off-center, generous breathing space for headline overlay.
Lighting, dramatic cinematic lighting, single key light, atmospheric haze, golden-hour-like quality but in green-deep tonality.
Color palette, Paradeyes signature, deep green #003135 dominant, electric green #57EEA1 as secondary accent in light sources or rim lighting, dark bluish #14222E for shadows and depth, warm white #FAFAF7 for primary highlights only.
Texture, fine cinematic grain, subtle anamorphic flares, atmospheric particles.
Mood, confident, sophisticated, cinema-grade, premium agency vibe.
Style references, Apple product launch visuals, Linear company hero images, Vercel framer hero, modern Awwwards 2024 hero images, AirPods Pro launch page.
NO text overlay, NO logos visible, NO real human portraits.

## Brief utilisateur

{USER_BRIEF}

## Suffixe charte

Strict Paradeyes palette only. Cinematic premium quality. The image must work both standalone and as background for white text overlay.
